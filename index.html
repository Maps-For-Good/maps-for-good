Maps for Good!

Find your way to a better world.

Hey! We're Maps For Good, a student-led development team based in Worcester, MA. Our mission is to create a platform that allows disabled users to confidently navigate the world. Finding your way around a new place can be daunting, especially if you have to account for accessibility. We aim to make nearby amenities like benches, handicap restrooms, and accessible parking spaces easy to find. Additionally, we want users to interact with the map and share their experiences with others. If a toilet is out of order, for example, it's important that other people know to avoid it. With your help, Maps for Good hopes to make the world a little safer for everyone!

For any questions, concerns, or suggestions, please contact us at rahmed@wpi.edu, asun@wpi.edu, or ichakravarthy@wpi.edu.
